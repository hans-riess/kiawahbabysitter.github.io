About

We are a concierge childcare service operating in Kiawah Island, South Carlolina and the surrounding Charleston area.

We work with children of all ages,  and with families, both local and visiting.

While saftey is our number one priority, we also emphasize FUN! While the adults are going out to dinner or the golf course, your children are on vacation too  

Please inquire about availability and pricing, or use our inquiry form.

Who We Are

We are a growing network of young professionals in the Charleston area with a passion for childcare. 

Rebecca Riess, Founder

 

Hi, I'm Rebecca! You can call me Becca or Becky, I answer to all three! I founded Kiawah Babysitter because I have a passion working with kids. Growing up in New Jersey, I taught dance to 5-10 year-olds and was a manager at an ice cream shop at the Shore, mentoring kids 14 and older. My husband and his family have been vacationing on Kiawah since before 1994. In 2022, we got married on the beach there. So I guess you can say I "married into" Kiawah!
